1 You ask
👤 You
Ask in plain English: “Should I hold SUI for 2 weeks? (moderate risk)”
▼
🛡️ Question check
Really an investing question? Off-topic → polite redirect, never a fake answer.
One plain-English question in.
Everything to the right is automatic — and provable.
2 GlassBox decides
🧊 Freeze the facts
5 real market numbers locked in: trend · RSI · volatility · liquidity · drawdown
↓ pulled live from ↓
CoinGecko
live price, trend, RSI, volatility
DeepBook v3
order-book depth & spread (liquidity)
▼
🟢 Bull agent
argues the optimistic case — using ONLY the frozen numbers
🔴 Bear agent
argues the cautious case — using ONLY the frozen numbers
⇆ debate + 1 rebuttal ⇆
Gemini / OpenRouter powers the agents’ reasoning
▼
⚖️ Risk Arbiter decides
weighs both arguments against your risk level → one call
3 GlassBox proves it
VERDICT — BUYHOLDAVOID + Signal Strength
(how decisive the evidence was — not a profit forecast)
▼
🔏 Sign it
ed25519 signature = a tamper-evident fingerprint of the decision
▼
⛓️ Store + anchor
Walrus
stores the signed record (blob)
Sui
registers the on-chain anchor object
▼
✅ Verify it yourself
your own phone 📱 recomputes the fingerprint & checks the signature — no GlassBox server needed
🚨 TAMPER DETECTED
change one character → the fingerprint no longer matches
Tamper-evident, not tamper-proof — GlassBox proves WHAT the AI decided and that no one changed it, not that the call is right.
